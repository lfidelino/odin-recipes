French Toast

The morning classic

Wake up to an irresistibly delicious breakfast that's super quick and easy to whip up using a few basic ingredients you likely already have in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup - All-purpose Flour
  • 1 cup - Milk
  • 1 pinch - Salt
  • 3 pieces - Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp - Ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp - White sugar
  • 12 slices - Bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Slowly whisk in the milk.
  3. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated.
  6. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
